GPS Lap Timer

A universal GPS based timer for racers to log their lap times based on the track they are visiting...

Why: The current market for GPS devices in the racing industry if far outdated. Numerous versions of nearly identical devices are produced to accomplish a common goal, recording the analytics of motorsport racers. Timers found on the market are often divided by vehicle type (Motorcycle, Ko-Kart, Car, etc). This redundancy can be avoided and resolved with a new design.
Pricing of these devices frequently turns away customers who want a simple GPS timer.

Features:
-User interface
-Speedometer
-Gyroscopic analytics
-Data Logging

Components:
-GPS Module
-Microcontroller
-Display
-Power Supply
-Gyro Sensor
-Enclosure
-Buttons.

Language/Software: Python, CAD.

My journey with motorcycles began at the age of 5, when I first hopped on a dirt bike and instantly fell in love with the rush of two-wheeled adventure. It's a delicate balance between aggression and restraint, knowing when to push the limits and when to exercise caution.

Despite the risks and challenges, motorcycle racing remains the ultimate source of happiness in my life. This mantra inspires me to chase perfection and continual improvement, both on and off the track.

Cars

Growing up, my fascination with cars ignited a lifelong passion for automotive culture. From a young age, I immersed myself in the world of automobiles, relishing the opportunity to learn about various makes and models. These qualities extend beyond the realm of automotive enthusiasts and positively influence my approach to challenges in both personal and professional endeavors.
